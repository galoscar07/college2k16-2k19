Description

An attendence is a entity that represents the physical presence of a Student at a Subject, held by a Teacher.

An Attendence contains also the information about Stundent's grade at that meeting.

We don't have the concept of a meeting, a student can go at any meeting and is the job of Teacher to Attend the Student with the correct WeekNumber and TypeOf meeting.
